Forgot Your Password?

Troubleshooting Login Issues

Here are common problems users face and how to fix them:

  • Incorrect credentials: Double-check your email and password for typos.
  • Browser issues: Clear cache or use a different browser if login fails to load.
  • Account locked: Too many failed attempts? Wait a few minutes or reset your password.
  • No access to email? Contact Idrive support to recover your account securely.

Best Practices for a Secure Login

  • Always use a strong and unique password for Idrive.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) if available.
  • Log out after each session on public or shared devices.
  • Avoid using outdated browsers that may compromise security.
